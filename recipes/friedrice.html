Chicken and Rice Recipe

Description

A comforting and wholesome dish featuring tender, seasoned chicken pieces nestled on a bed of fluffy, aromatic rice. The chicken is typically seasoned with a blend of herbs and spices, such as garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and thyme, enhancing its flavor. The rice, often cooked in chicken broth for an extra layer of richness, absorbs the savory juices from the chicken as it cooks, resulting in a flavorful and satisfying one-pot meal.

Garnished with fresh herbs like parsley or cilantro, this dish is not only delicious but also a perfect balance of protein and carbohydrates. It's a versatile recipe that can be easily customized to suit different culinary preferences.

Ingridients:

  • Chicken pieces (thighs, drumsticks, or breasts)
  • Rice
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika

Steps:

  1. Season & Sear Chicken: Season chicken with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add Rice and Broth: Stir in rice to coat with flavorful oils.
  3. Simmer: Bring to a boil, then simmer covered for 15-20 minutes until rice is cooked, and chicken is done.
  4. Garnish and Serve: Fluff rice with a fork, garnish with fresh herbs, and serve warm.
