lasagna Recipe

Description

Layers of wide pasta sheets, rich and savory homemade meat sauce with ground beef and Italian sausage, interspersed with creamy ricotta and melted mozzarella cheese. Each layer is generously seasoned with a blend of garlic, basil, oregano, and a touch of red pepper flakes for a hint of warmth.

Baked to perfection, the result is a comforting and hearty Italian dish, with crispy edges and a gooey, cheesy center that promises to satisfy your taste buds. Serve hot, and enjoy the delicious melding of flavors in every bite.

Ingridients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Italian sausage
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Egg (for binding the ricotta)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps:

    Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    Cook Lasagna Noodles: Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
    Prepare Meat Sauce: Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste
    Prepare Ricotta Mixture: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and a beaten egg.
    Assemble the Lasagna: In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce.
    Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
    Rest and Garnish: Allow the lasagna to rest for about 10 minutes before slicing.
    Serve: Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
